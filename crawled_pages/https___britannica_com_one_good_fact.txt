Title: One Good Fact about Lunar Pranks | Britannica
H1: During the Apollo 12 Moon landing, astronauts had small checklists attached to the cuff of their space suits so that they could quickly access mission details on the lunar surface. Members of the Apollo 12 crew pranked the astronauts by including Playboy photos of nude models among the checklists.
